Introducing: NEW CUTTING-EDGE TECHNOLOGY!

What new technologies can help reduce our carbon footprint?

AIR-INK

AIR-INK is a special kind of high-quality ink made from air pollution.

PROS

AIR-INK gets rid of air pollution by repurposing it as ink.

CONS

AIR-INK is dependent on the fact that there is pollution in the air, and so it is very limited.

Edible Water Bottles

This "water bottle" is really just a clear pouch filled with water that humans can digest.

PROS

It is bio-degradable and reduces litter (people would just eat the water bottle once they are done).

CONS

This watter bottle is a choking hazard and should not be given to small children.

LifeStraw

This is a special straw that filters out germs and makes any water directly drinkable.

PROS

This straw could save many lives by giving people drinking water at any time.

CONS

The straw is not totally reliable and you still can get sick if you're not careful.

Drinkable Sea Water (Salt Filter)

This invention filters the salt out of sea water, making it drinkable.

PROS

Ocean water covers 71% of the Earth, so we won't be running out of drinking water anytime soon!

CONS

This specific method kills sea life and is not 100% reliable.
